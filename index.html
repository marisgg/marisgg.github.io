Maris F. L. Galesloot

ELLIS PhD Candidate @ Radboud University Nijmegen.

Affiliated with the Department of Software Science (SWS) of the Institute for Computing and Information Sciences (iCIS), located at Radboud University in Nijmegen, the Netherlands.

Supervised by Prof. Dr. Nils Jansen, part of the AI-FM research group and member of the coordinator team of the ELLIS Unit Nijmegen. My ELLIS co-supervisor is Prof. Dr. Nick Hawes from the GOALS research group of the Oxford Robotics Institute located at the University of Oxford in the United Kingdom. Hence, I was a visiting PhD student at the University of Oxford between February and August 2025.

My research interests revolve around sequential decision-making under uncertainty. More specifically, my PhD research specializes in (online) planning under uncertainty and model-based reinforcement learning with a touch of probabilistic verification for partially observable and uncertain environments. Such environments are typically modelled by (robust) extensions or (proper) subsets of partially observable Markov decision processes (POMDPs). My work focuses on developing scalable and robust algorithms and aims to combine the robustness of model-based approaches with the scalability of data-driven learning techniques.
